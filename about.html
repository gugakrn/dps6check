About DPS6Check

DPS6Check is an independent educational project created to provide clear and structured information about the Ford DPS6 (Powershift) dual-clutch transmission.

The purpose of this website is to help vehicle owners better understand how certain transmission parameters behave over time, based on learned values and usage patterns commonly observed in real-world scenarios.

This project is not affiliated with Ford Motor Company or any vehicle manufacturer. All content published here is based on technical study, practical experience and publicly available information.

Any tools or reference calculators available on this website are strictly educational in nature. They do not measure physical wear, do not provide diagnosis and do not replace professional mechanical inspection or official service procedures.

DPS6Check was created to promote technical understanding, responsible ownership and informed decision-making, avoiding misinformation and oversimplified claims often found online.
